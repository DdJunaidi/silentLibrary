Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a library card?
Visit our circulation desk with a valid ID and proof of address. Library cards are free for all residents.

Can I renew books online?
Yes! Use our online portal or call the circulation desk to renew your items.

Do you offer printing services?
Yes, we offer black & white printing for $0.10 per page and color printing for $0.50 per page.

Can I reserve meeting rooms?
Yes, library cardholders can reserve study rooms and meeting spaces up to 2 weeks in advance.

Do you have WiFi?
Free WiFi is available throughout the library. The network name is "SilentLibrary-Free".

Are there late fees?
Yes, late fees are $0.25 per day for regular books and $1.00 per day for DVDs and new releases.
