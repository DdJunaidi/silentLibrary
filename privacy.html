Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

Information We Collect

Silent Library is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect information necessary to provide library services, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number, email)
  • Library card number and borrowing history
  • Computer and internet usage logs (when using library computers)
  • Event attendance records

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide library services and maintain your account
  • Send overdue notices and library communications
  • Improve our services and programs
  • Comply with legal requirements

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or court order
  • To collection agencies for overdue materials
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Children's Privacy

We take special care to protect the privacy of children under 13. Parental consent is required for children's library accounts, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental permission.

Website Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website and take effect immediately upon posting.
